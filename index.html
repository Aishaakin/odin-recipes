Recipe Chilli

Description

Homemade chili is delicious and goes great with cornbread or over corn chips for a chili pie! I like to use spicy pinto beans. The recepie is called Home-Made Chilli.

Ingredients

The ingredients to make the chilli food are:

  • Pasta
  • Sliced Meats
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices
How to make the Homemade Chilli
  1. Cook the beef and the onion.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients, then bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce the heat and let simmer for 15 minutes.
